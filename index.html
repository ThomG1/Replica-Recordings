ABOUT

Welcome to the website for Replica Recordings. We are an independent record label established in June 2020. As a label, we were formed around electronic music, but we aren’t tethered to one particular genre and aim to release our favourite of records from the South Wales underground each year. This website is a place where you can keep up to date on news and releases from our artists and DJs, as well as a calendar of upcoming events we’d love you to be a part of. If you’d like to create with us, feel free to send us a demo and we’ll get back to you when we can.
