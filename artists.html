Carapace is an electronic music producer based in Cardiff. The first signing to Replica Recordings back in 2020, Carapace has as subsequently released three EP’s, and a string of dark, neo-psychedelic singles on the label and only looks to be getting started, with a further EP scheduled for later in the year.

Trypt is a multi -instrumentalist hip hop producer best known for dreamlike ambient cuts over classic hip hop breaks and contemporary production. Debuting on Replica Recordings with the liminal ‘Detour’ single and following collaboration with Carapace ‘Without Shadows’ Trypt has cemented themselves as a key figure in the scene over the last year and we are so excited to see what’s next.

MFurreed is a DJ/MC from Merthyr Tydfil, and the first DJ signing to Replica. The inaugural mix of the Replica Mix series flows effortlessly from house and hip hop into techno and jungle and manages to encapsulate everything we love about music in under an hour.

BabaYaga takes everything we usually look for as a label, and manages to subvert expectations completely through their abstract, dynamic alternative rock. After a decade of shows around the country, they perform with an energy impossible to imitate and have releases ‘Shade’ and ‘Down’ coming out on Replica Recordings later in the year.
